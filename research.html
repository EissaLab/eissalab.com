A

Behavior

Online, crowdsourced video games with hidden features allow us to explore the types of cognitive strategies humans use in complex and dynamic environments.

B

Models

Mathematical and computational models, spanning behavioral and neural network models help use uncover mechanisms on how different strategies are implemented by the brain.

C

Neural Activity

Human brain recordings, including intracranial recordings of neural populations, help us interpret which mechanisms are biologically relevant.
